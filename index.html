📖 Rulebook

Overview

2 players · 30 HP each · 3 rounds · 6 shots per round. The player with the most HP after all rounds wins.

⚔ Weapons & Damage

💣 TNT11 dmg
🪓 Axe10 dmg
🔨 Mace9 dmg
🏹 Bow8 dmg
⚔️ Sword7 dmg
🔱 Trident6 dmg
🎯 Crossbow5 dmg

🎯 Each Shot — Two Phases

  1. Player A secretly picks a weapon & a shield value (5–11)
  2. Player B guesses Player A's weapon, then picks their own weapon & shield (5–11)
  3. Both players attack each other simultaneously

💥 Damage Formula

Damage taken = |Your Shield − Opponent's Weapon Damage|

Perfectly matching your shield to your opponent's weapon deals 0 damage (Perfect Block!). Both over- and under-estimating are equally punished.

🔒 Weapon Restriction

Only 6 of 7 weapons are available per round. Once either player uses a weapon, it is locked for the rest of that round.

🏆 Winning & Sudden Death

After 3 rounds, highest HP wins. On a tie, a Sudden Death round (6 more shots) decides the winner.

🎮 Game Modes

  • Hot Seat — Same device. After Player A picks, the screen blanks and you pass the device to Player B.
  • Online — Create a room, share the 6-letter code, play on separate devices in real-time.
  • vs AI — Solo play. The AI picks randomly from available weapons — can you read it?

📖 Rulebook

Overview

2 players · 30 HP each · 3 rounds · 6 shots per round. Most HP after all rounds wins.

⚔ Weapons & Tiers

There are 5 tiers of weapons. Tier 1 is free. Unlock higher tiers in the Shop.

T1 Starter5–6 dmg · Free
T2 Iron7–8 dmg · 80 🪙
T3 Steel9–10 dmg · 200 🪙
T4 Shadow11–12 dmg · 400 🪙
T5 Legendary13–15 dmg · 800 🪙

Equip up to 6 weapons in the Loadout settings. Your equipped weapons determine which shield values you can choose.

🎯 Each Shot

  1. Player A secretly picks a weapon & shield value (5–11)
  2. Player B picks their own weapon & shield
  3. Both players attack simultaneously

💥 Damage Formula

Damage = |Your Shield − Opponent's Weapon Damage|

Match your shield exactly = 0 damage (Perfect Block!).

🧪 Potions

Buy potions in the Shop for 15 🪙 each. During combat, use one instead of attacking to restore +10 HP. You are still vulnerable to your opponent's attack.

✨ Special Shot (0.1% chance)

Any shot has a 0.1% chance to become a Special Shot. Players alternate guessing each other's hidden weapon. First to 3 correct guesses wins 30 🪙. Normal combat resumes after.

💀 Boss Battle

Both players team up against The Warlord (60 HP). Attack the boss every shot — the player who lands the killing blow wins 20 🪙. The boss hits back each shot with a random weapon.

🪙 Tokens

  • Win a match → +200 🪙
  • Lose a match → +50 🪙
  • Special Round win → +30 🪙
  • Boss kill → +400 🪙

🏆 Winning & Sudden Death

After 3 rounds, most HP wins. On a tie, a Sudden Death round decides it.

🎮 Game Modes

  • Hot Seat — Same device. Screen blanks after Player A picks, pass to Player B.
  • Online — Create a room, share the 6-letter code, play on separate devices.
  • vs AI — AI picks randomly each shot.
  • Boss Battle — Co-op vs The Warlord. Killing blow earns 20 🪙.

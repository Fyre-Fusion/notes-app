📖 Rulebook

Overview

2 players · 30 HP each · 3 rounds · 6 shots per round. Most HP after all rounds wins.

⚔ Weapons & Tiers

5 tiers of weapons. T1 starters are free. Max 12 weapons equipped per loadout.

T1 Starter5–10 dmg · Free · 12 slots default
T2 Iron10–11 dmg · 80 🪙
T3 Steel11–12 dmg · 200 🪙
T4 Shadow13 dmg · 400 🪙
T5 Legendary14 dmg · 800 🪙
T6 Divine15 dmg · 1000 🪙 · Gentuga Tensho

When you equip a weapon with a new damage value, that shield value is added to your opponent — they can now block it.

🎯 Each Shot

  1. Player A secretly picks a weapon & shield value
  2. Player B picks their own weapon & shield
  3. Both players attack simultaneously

💥 Damage Formula

Damage = |Your Shield − Opponent's Weapon Damage|

Match your shield exactly = 0 damage (Perfect Block!).

🧪 Potions

Buy potions in the Shop for 15 🪙 each. Restore +10 HP per use. Max 9.

✨ Special Shot (0.1% chance)

Guess opponent's hidden weapon. First to 3 correct guesses wins 30 🪙.

💀 Boss Battle

Both players team up against The Warlord (60 HP). Player who lands the killing blow wins 300 🪙.

🪙 Tokens

  • Win a match → +200 🪙
  • Lose a match → +50 🪙
  • Special Round win → +30 🪙
  • Boss kill → +300 🪙

⭐ XP & Levels

Earn XP by playing. XP uses compound interest — the higher your level, the more XP each action earns. Unlock badges at each level from Rookie → Grandmaster.

✨ Trait Shop

Spend 500 🪙 to roll a random trait on any weapon. Traits have rarities up to 0.00% chance. 25 unique traits available.
